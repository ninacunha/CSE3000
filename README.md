# AI Ethics 
* **Fairness and Antidiscrimination** - We will design and deploy AI systems that treat all individuals equitably, regardless of race, gender, age, or background. We actively identify and mitigate bias in data, models, and outcomes.
* **Transparency** - We will be open about when and how AI is used in our products and decisions. Stakeholders will always know they are interacting with an AI system and understand its purpose.
* **Accountability and Respect** - We take full responsibility for the outcomes of our AI systems and hold ourselves to the highest ethical standards. We treat every person affected by our technology with dignity and respect.
* **Explainabilty** - We will strive to make AI decisions and recommendations understandable to the people they affect. Where full technical explanation is not possible, we will provide meaningful context and reasoning.
* **Accuracy and Reliability** - We are committed to building AI systems that perform consistently, accurately, and dependably across all use cases. We continuously test, monitor, and improve our models to maintain trust.
* **Security, Safety and Privacy** - We will protect user data, prevent misuse, and build AI systems that are secure by design. The safety of our users and communities is a non-negotiable priority in every deployment decision.
* **Beneficence** - We develop AI with the explicit goal of generating positive value for individuals, organizations, and society. We weigh potential harms carefully and will not deploy AI where benefits do not clearly outweigh risks.
* **Human control and human centric design** - Humans remain in meaningful control of AI-assisted decisions at all times. Our systems are designed to augment human judgment, not replace it.
